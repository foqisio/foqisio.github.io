One-Click Contextual Sharing

Make sharing drop-dead simple for your readers with Foqis One-Click Contextual Sharing!

When your readers highlight any text in an article, they will be presented with an unobtrusive contextual sharing dialog for social platforms like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn and more.

Or, your readers can simply highlight text as they would do in a book and save it to their Foqis Saved Notes.

As a publisher, discover how many readers are highlighting, saving and sharing your different parts of your articles and choose to display those statistics inline!
