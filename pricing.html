How difficult is setup?

Setting up Foqis is as simple as adding a snippet of Javascript to your site. It shouldn't take more than a couple minutes at the most… and don't worry, our documenation makes the process very easy for anyone to follow!

Is Foqis really free?

Every core feature is included in the free plan. If you begin to approach the pageview limit for your plan, we will send you an email to let you know before reaching the limit!

What does Foqis do with my data?

Any data we collect is only used internally to improve the Foqis offerings and customer experience. We will never sell or give data we collect to any other party for any reason.
